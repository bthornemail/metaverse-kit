# Hitecture Preservation License (HPL) - Draft

This license is an intent statement until finalized. It focuses on preserving the
human and social architecture of the system: identity, authorship, and attribution.

## Core Requirements

1. Preserve author attribution and provenance of events.
2. Preserve auditability of changes through append-only traces.
3. Do not remove or obscure origin metadata in derived works.
4. Clearly disclose modifications and divergences from the original project.

## Allowances

- You may study, fork, and experiment with the code and ideas.
- You may build compatible implementations that retain the requirements above.

## Restrictions

- You may not claim authorship of the original work.
- You may not present derivative work as the original project.
