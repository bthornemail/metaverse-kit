    - Covariant: they move/scale along with the observer’s reference frame.

    - Represented as smaller balls inside or around the ideals.
        
    - Contravariant: they respond inversely to changes in the observer’s frame.

    - Shared observations arise when their covariant and contravariant spheres **overlap consistently**.
        
5. **Regular Polyhedra → Ideal & Idol Variables**
    
    - **Vertices → Ideals**
        
    - **Edges → Idols**
        
    - Shared polyhedron between observers = **mutually valid, intersubjective mapping**.
        
6. **Tetrahedron of Covariant/Contravariant Variables**
    
    - Minimal unit of mutually agreed observation.

4. **Tetrahedron → Minimal Mutual Observation**:

5. **Polyhedron → Full Observer Mapping**:
    
    - Vertices = ideals (conceptual points)
